User Guide

Site Mechanics

Navigate this archive of 432 artifacts using these tools:

  • Browse Gallery: Use the sidebar Facets to filter items by year, creator, or document type.
  • Timeline: Follow the visual evolution of dissent across the Reagan and Bush administrations.
  • Map View: Click city markers to explore regional DIY networks and local scenes.
  • Metadata Search: Use the search bar for specific keywords (e.g., "Nuclear," "Police," "Zine").

Archival Sources

These primary sources are held in the Cometbus Punk and Underground Press Collection at Cornell University.

  • Source: Rare and Manuscript Collections.
  • Location: Box 4 (Box 8107).
  • Context: Each digital record contains a "Location" field cross-referencing its physical folder.
